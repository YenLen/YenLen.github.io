KeMana

KeMana is a bus app for Jakarta bus system which was designed by me when I worked as UI Art Design Intern in CHOCOLABS "KeMana" in Indonesian language means "Where are you going?" KeMana app tells the users how long will it takes to wait for the bus.
I joined KeMana as UI designer at the stage when product manager had already done the basic research, wireframe for the product. My job was to map out the userflow, develop the overall user experience and create the visual interface. I follow the Material Design to design this Android app.

What to solve?

Our product manager is an Indonesian. Whenever she went back to Jarkata, she found bus stops was crowded with people to get on the bus. In Jakarta, buses are mainly use as a means of transportation. We want to provide a service that tell users "where is the bus", "when will the bus arrive", and "how to transfer for their destination". We think it can reduce citizens' waiting time as long as citizens know the above information.
