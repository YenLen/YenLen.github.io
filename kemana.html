KeMana

KeMana is a bus app for Jakarta bus system which was designed by myself when I worked as a UI Art Design Intern at CHOCOLABS (merged into LINE TV).
I joined KeMana team as UI designer at the stage when product manager had already done the basic research and wireframe of the product. My jobs were to map out the user flow, develop the overall user experience and create the visual user interface by following Material Design guidelines.

What to solve?
In Jakarta, buses are used as their major means of transportation and always crowded with people. We want to provide a service that tells users "where is the bus now", "when will the bus arrive", and "how to transfer to reach their destinations". We believed it can help to reduce users' waiting time by providing the above information.
