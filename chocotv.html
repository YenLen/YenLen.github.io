CHOCO TV

CHOCO TV was one of the projects I joined when working at CHOCOLABS as a UI Art Design Intern. I redesigned part of the user interface of android version to provide a better user experience.

What to solve?

More and more Taiwanese slide their mobile phones when commuting. What are they looking at with their phones? Mostly, they are browsing social media, news, watching drama! Mobile phones turn to be a common device for people watching dramas. Thus, CHOCO TV mainly wants to provide a convenient way for people watching dramas with their mobile devices. Moreover, CHOCO TV is a free app and providing free dramas as well.


Role: UI Designer
Type: Mobile App
Year: 2015
