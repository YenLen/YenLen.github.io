CHOCO TV

CHOCO TV CHOCO TV was one of projects I joined when working at CHOCOLABS as UI Art Design Intern. I redesigned part of User Interface of android version to provide a better user experience.

What to solve?

More and more Taiwanese browses mobile phone when commuting. What do people usually do when taking the MRT? Mostly, Browsing social media, news, watching drama! Mobile phone turns to be a common device for people watching dramas. Thus, CHOCO TV mainly wants to provide a convenient way for people watching dramas with their mobile device. Moreover, CHOCO TV is a free app and providing free dramas as well.


Role: UI Designer
Type: Mobile App
Year: 2015
