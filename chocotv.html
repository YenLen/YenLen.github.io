CHOCO TV

CHOCO TV was one of the leading streaming platforms in Taiwan with the aim of providing better video watching experience. When working at CHOCOLABS (merged into LINE TV) as a UI Design Intern. I redesigned the user interface of following features in android version and develop a better user experience.


What to solve?
Mobile phones turned to be the most used devices by modern people when watching videos. Therefore, CHOCO TV mainly wants to provide a more convenient way for people watching dramas with their mobile devices.


Role: UI Designer
Type: Mobile App
Year: 2015

Produced by CHOCOLABS
Product Manager: Rayon Hung
Designer: Yen-Len Chen, Cindy Huang, Celyn Tu
